No Scary Parts

Automatically skip the scary scenes in family movies from Disney+, so your little ones can enjoy the characters and music.

Add to Chrome It's Free

How It Works

While playing a movie, scenes to be skipped are highlighted in the progress bar. It's that easy.

Scenes with overly dramatic, traumatic, or just plain creepy moments are skipped, while maintaining continuity as much as possible.

Supported Titles

When you browse Disney+, supported titles are marked next to the play button with the icon .

Currently supported titles are listed below, with more on the way. Make your requests here.
